[ about Us ]

Best Interior Solutions

Vinod Talreja and Sahana Vasudevan are accomplished civil engineers who graduated from M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore. However, their passion for transforming homes led them to pursue an advanced diploma in interior design. Prior to establishing their own practice, they held various positions in leading builders in Bangalore, such as Total Environment, Brigade Group, and Adarsh Developers. With over two decades of experience in the industry, they bring a refined and hands-on-ground approach to design and build projects. They value their relationship with their clients and find motivation in providing them with exceptional service. When not working, they enjoy birdwatching, photography, and attending art exhibitions, which inspire their work. It is no surprise that they have won numerous awards and garnered the admiration of many.

From Sketch to Life

We get to know you?
We start by meeting with you to discuss your dreams for the space. We gather measurements, document all the little quirks, and get to know your personal style. Whether in person or via video chat, we take the time to understand your needs and vision-all while sticking to your budget
We put our skills to work.
Next, we put our heads together to develop an original design concept and color palette. Once we've curated the best ideas from each of our designers, we start sourcing some of the key pieces. In just a few days, we send you a digital design appetizer to give you a taste of what we've got cooking. Give us foodback, and we'll tailor the design to moot your needs!
