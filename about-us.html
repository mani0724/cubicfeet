[ about Us ]

Best Interior Solutions

Vinod Talreja and Sahana Vasudevan are accomplished civil engineers who graduated from M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore. However, their passion for transforming homes led them to pursue an advanced diploma in interior design.

Prior to establishing their own practice, they held various positions in leading builders in Bangalore, such as Total Environment, Brigade Group, and Adarsh Developers.

With over two decades of experience in the industry, they bring a refined and hands-on-ground approach to design and build projects. They value their relationship with their clients and find motivation in providing them with exceptional service.

When not working, they enjoy birdwatching, photography, and attending art exhibitions, which inspire their work. It is no surprise that they have won numerous awards and garnered the admiration of many.

[ how it works ]

From Sketch to Life

We get to know you
We start by meeting with you to discuss your dreams for the space. We gather measurements, document all the little quirks, and get to know your personal style. Whether in person or via video chat, we take the time to understand your needs and vision-all while sticking to your budget
We put our skills together
Next, we put our heads together to develop an original design concept and color palette. Once we've curated the best ideas from each of our designers, we start sourcing some of the key pieces. In just a few days, we send you a digital design appetizer to give you a taste of what we've got cooking. Give us feedback, and we'll tailor the design to meet your needs!
We Deliver
We work through a combination of creative design, project management, and coordination with other professionals. By working closely with our client and other stakeholders throughout the process, we are able to create beautiful, functional, and comfortable spaces that meet the needs of our clients. Once the design concept is approved, our designer will begin developing the design in more detail. This includes selecting specific furniture, fixtures, and finishes for the space, as well as creating detailed drawings and plans. The designer may also work with other professionals, such as architects or contractors, to ensure that the design is feasible and meets building codes and regulations. The designer may also visit the site regularly to oversee the construction and ensure that the design is being implemented correctly.
